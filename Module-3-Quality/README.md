# Module 3 – Quality (CMC)

## Purpose
Module 3 provides detailed **Chemistry, Manufacturing,
and Controls (CMC)** information to ensure the identity,
strength, quality, and purity of the drug product.

## 3.2.S – Drug Substance
- General information
- Manufacturing process and process controls
- Control of materials
- Characterization
- Control of drug substance
- Reference standards
- Stability

## 3.2.P – Drug Product
- Description and composition
- Pharmaceutical development
- Manufacturing process and controls
- Control of excipients
- Control of drug product
- Reference standards
- Container closure system
- Stability

## Regulatory Importance
- Core module for regulatory review
- Supports product approval and lifecycle management
- Reviewed heavily during variations and inspections
